Who Are We?

We are a group of students interested in technology and programming. YNC_Hacks was created as a hackerspace for this group of students to learn and apply their technology skills.

Our Values

  • Clean Code
  • Active Participation
  • Fun and Laughs

Our Partners

We hope to have a variety of partners in the upcoming semester, stay tuned!

What do We Offer?

YNC_Hacks offers two tracks, one for training and one for participation in projects. Members are required to participate in either track, and spend the semester either honing their programming and design skills, or to apply these skills for projects.
